“The Hobbit” by J.R.R. Tolkien is a timeless adventure that follows Bilbo Baggins, a humble hobbit who is swept into a daring quest with thirteen dwarves to reclaim their lost treasure from the dragon Smaug. Along the journey, Bilbo faces trolls, goblins, and giant spiders, discovering courage and wit he never knew he had. Filled with magic, humor, and heart, it’s a tale of unexpected heroism and the beauty of stepping beyond one’s comfort zone.
